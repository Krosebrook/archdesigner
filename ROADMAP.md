## üìù Notes

### Assumptions
- Development team of 5-10 engineers
- Agile/Scrum methodology
- 2-week sprints
- Continuous deployment
- User feedback loops every sprint

### Constraints
- Technical debt paydown: 20% of capacity
- Bug fixes and maintenance: 15% of capacity
- Innovation time: 10% of capacity
- Net feature development: 55% of capacity

### Risks & Mitigations
- **Risk**: Scope creep in complex features
  - **Mitigation**: Strict MVP definitions, feature flags

- **Risk**: Third-party API dependencies
  - **Mitigation**: Fallback mechanisms, vendor diversification

- **Risk**: User adoption of advanced features
  - **Mitigation**: Progressive disclosure, excellent onboarding
